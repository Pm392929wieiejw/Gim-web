About Us

The Group of Islamic Members (GIM) is dedicated to protecting Muslims and their rights. We actively combat Islamophobia and counter harmful narratives against Islam, both online and in real life. Our mission includes spreading awareness, fostering unity among Muslims, and taking decisive action against those who seek to harm our community. Through strategic measures, we protect the dignity of Islam and its followers.
